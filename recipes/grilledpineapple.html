Grilled Pineapple

Description

This grilled pineapple dish takes around 45 minutes to make. wild stuff. - By lebron james the III

Ingredients

  • One fresh pineapple, cut into rings
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon of honey
  • 1 dash of hot pepper sauce
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Place pineapple rings into a large resealable plastic bag. Add the butter, honey, hot pepper sauce, and salt into this bag.
  2. Seal, then shake the bag to coat evenly. Let this marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil grate. Grill pineapple rings until grill marks appear - around 2-3 minutes each side
