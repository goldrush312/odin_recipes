Cottage Cheese

Description

It really is cottage cheese. This one could be cottage cheese. It kinda looks like cottage cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of vinegar
  • 1 pinch salt to taste

Steps

  1. Pour milk into a small pot and boil. Add vinegar and remove from heat. Stir pot slowly until curds form - 1-2 minutes.
  2. Pour mixture into a colander lined with cheesecloth. Allow liquid whey to drain off as curds cool. Season the curds with salt.
